We do it all!

The team at Pongakawa Motors are fully qualified mechanics specialising in various areas of automotive repair and maintenance.
We offer a large array of service from Diagnostic,
Engine Rebuild, Transmission Specialist, Brake, Air Conditioning, Electrical Systems, Fuel System, Exhaust System Specialist, Tire and Wheel, Performance Tuner, Classic Car Restorer, Collision Repair, Auto Body Painter, Auto Detailer, Full Rust Treatment and Diesel Engine.

Our Services

Diagnostic

We specialise in diagnosing and identifying issues using advanced diagnostic tools and computer systems.

Engine Rebuild

We focus on rebuilding and repairing engines, including engine block machining, cylinder head reconditioning, and piston replacement.

Transmission

We specialise in diagnosing and repairing transmission problems, including manual and automatic transmissions.

Brake

We repair and maintain brake systems, including brake pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines.

Suspension & Steering

We also specialise in repairing and maintaining suspension systems, shocks, struts, and steering components.

Air Conditioning

We focus on repairing and maintaining the heating, ventilation, HVAC and air conditioning systems in vehicles.

Electrical

We can diagnose and repair electrical issues, including wiring problems, battery issues, and electronic component failures.

Fuel System

Fuel System Technician: Focuses on repairing and maintaining fuel injection systems, carburetors, and fuel pumps.

Exhaust System

Exhaust System Specialist: Specializes in repairing and maintaining exhaust systems, including mufflers, catalytic converters, and exhaust pipes.

Tyres & Wheels

Tyres and Wheel Technician: Focuses on tire mounting, balancing, alignment, and repairing damaged wheels.

Performance

Performance Tuner: Specializes in modifying and tuning engines for increased performance, including upgrades to intake, exhaust, and engine control systems.

Classic Car

Classic Car Restorer: Focuses on restoring and repairing classic and vintage cars to their original condition.

Collision Repair

Collision Repair Technician: Specializes in repairing vehicles that have been damaged in accidents, including bodywork, frame straightening, and painting.

Body Painter

Auto Body Painter: Focuses on painting and refinishing vehicles, ensuring a smooth and even finish.

Auto Detailer

Auto Detailer: Specializes in cleaning and detailing vehicles, including interior cleaning, exterior polishing, and waxing.

Rust Proofing Treatment

We offer a full range of rust-proofing services for all kinds of vehicles. From applying an underbody and cavity wax or epoxy paint to the chassis, we have the experience and the tools to provide you with the right treatment for your vehicle.

Diesel Engine

Diesel Engine Technician: Specializes in repairing and maintaining diesel engines, commonly found in trucks, buses, and some passenger vehicles.

Fleet Maintenance

Fleet Maintenance Technician: Focuses on maintaining and repairing vehicles in commercial fleets, such as delivery trucks and company cars.

Race Car

Race Car Mechanic: Specializes in preparing and maintaining race cars for competitive events, focusing on performance and reliability.

Auto Glass

Auto Glass Technician: Focuses on repairing and replacing automotive glass, including windshields, windows, and mirrors.
